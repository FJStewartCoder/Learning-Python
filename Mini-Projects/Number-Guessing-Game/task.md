## Details

Rating: 2/10  
Random numbers are the hardest part of this.

## Brief
Create a simple game where you have to guess the number.  

Generate a random number between and including 1 - 100.  
You will have 6 guesses to guess the number.  
Tell the player if they need to guess higher or lower.  

If the player wins, inform them.
If they lose (all 6 turns are used) tell them the answer.
